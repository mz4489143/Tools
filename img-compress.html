How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor lets you reduce the file size of your images while maintaining good quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the "Select Images" button
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher values mean more compression)
  3. Select your desired output format (or keep the original format)
  4. Click "Compress Images" to start the process
  5. Download your optimized images when the process is complete

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience and SEO
  • Reduced storage space: Save storage on your devices and servers
  • Bandwidth savings: Use less data when sharing images online
  • Better performance: Optimized images work better on all devices
